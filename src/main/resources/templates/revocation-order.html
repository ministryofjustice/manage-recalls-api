Note:

 

An offender whose licence is revoked is liable to be detained in pursuance of their sentence and may be apprehended without warrant. If they remain at large they are deemed to be unlawfully at large and any period for which they remain at large does not count towards the completion of their sentence.  Their sentence end date will be deferred to take account of the period they were unlawfully at large.
